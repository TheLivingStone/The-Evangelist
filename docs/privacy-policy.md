# Privacy Policy — The Evangelist

_Last updated: July 1, 2026_

The Evangelist ("the app", "we", "us") helps believers track and share their
evangelism and outreach. This policy explains what we collect, why, and your
choices. If you have questions, contact us at **cjctechgrace@gmail.com**.

## Who runs this app

The Evangelist is operated by Oliyad Deyasa.
Our backend is hosted on Supabase, which stores your data securely on our behalf.

## What we collect

We only collect what the app needs to work. We do **not** sell your data, and we
do **not** use it for advertising or cross-app tracking.

- **Account information** — your email address and name, used to create and
  secure your account. If you sign in with Apple, we receive the name and email
  you choose to share (you may use Apple's "Hide My Email").
- **Approximate location** — when you enable it, your approximate location
  centers the nearby outreach map and, only during an active outreach with map
  visibility turned on, shows your approximate position to others. Locations are
  deliberately blurred ("fuzzed") so your exact location is never shown.
- **Location when you add a person** — if you allow it, the app also saves the
  coordinates of where you were when you added a contact, stored with that
  contact as a private reminder of where you met them. This is separate from
  map visibility, is never shown to other users, and is only ever visible to
  your church if you separately choose to share that specific contact.
- **Content you create** — testimonies, outreach logs, prayers, posts,
  comments, contacts you add, and any photos you choose to attach.
- **Basic app/device information** — standard technical data (such as app
  version and device type) used to operate and troubleshoot the app.

We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

## How we use your information

- To provide core features: your dashboard, the community feed, the map, and
  saving the people and activities you log.
- To secure your account and prevent abuse.
- To respond to your support requests.

## How your information is shared

- **With other users:** content you choose to post to the community feed, and
  your name/approximate position on the map when you make yourself visible
  during an outreach, are visible to other signed-in users.
- **With your church (only if you choose):** if you've joined a church in the
  app and that church has confirmed your membership, you can choose to share
  an individual contact you've added — including who added them and when —
  with that church's leader, so the church can follow up. This is **off by
  default**, controlled per contact (with an optional default you can turn on
  in Settings), and only ever available to a church you've confirmed
  membership with.
- **With our service provider:** Supabase, which hosts our database and
  authentication. We do not sell or rent your data to anyone.
- **When required by law:** if we must comply with a legal obligation.

## Data retention and deletion

You can **delete your account at any time** from within the app
(Profile → Delete account). Deleting your account removes your profile and
associated personal data from our active systems. Some records may persist
briefly in backups before being overwritten.

## Your choices

- **Location:** you can turn location access on or off in your device settings
  at any time; the app works without it (the map simply won't center on you).
- **Photos:** photo access is only used when you choose to attach a photo.
- **Account:** you can edit your profile or delete your account in the app.
- **Church sharing:** if you belong to a church in the app, you decide
  whether each contact you add is visible to that church, either one at a
  time or by turning on a default in Settings. You can turn this off at any
  time, and leaving a church immediately stops that church from seeing your
  contacts.

## Security

We use industry-standard measures (including encrypted connections and access
controls) to protect your data. No method of transmission or storage is 100%
secure, but we work to protect your information.

## Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We'll update the "Last updated"
date above, and significant changes will be reflected in the app or on this page.
