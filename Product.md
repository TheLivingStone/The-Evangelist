# Evangelism App – Product Vision & Requirements Document (V1)

## Vision

To become the world's leading platform for encouraging Christians to actively share the Gospel, track their evangelism efforts, follow up with new believers, and connect people to local churches.

This app exists to help fulfill the Great Commission by making evangelism more visible, consistent, and sustainable.

The goal is not simply to track activity.

The goal is to encourage Christians to boldly share the Gospel and help new believers take their first steps in their walk with Christ.

---

# Mission Statement

To equip, encourage, and mobilize Christians around the world to share the Gospel through technology.

---

# Core Philosophy

The app is not:

- A Bible app

- A church management platform

- A devotional app

- A traditional social network

The app is:

- An evangelism platform

- A motivation system

- An outreach tracker

- A follow-up assistant

- A community of evangelists

Every feature must answer one question:

"Will this help more people share the Gospel?"

If the answer is no, the feature should not be prioritized.
